The Best Movies According to Bleir

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

I like Forrest Gump because it’s a heartwarming story about perseverance, love, and finding meaning in life, no matter the challenges you face.

The Patriot

I like The Patriot because it’s an intense, action-packed film that shows the strength of the human spirit, the sacrifices made during the American Revolution, and the powerful bond of family and freedom.

John Wick

I like John Wick because it’s a thrilling, high-intensity action film with a compelling, emotionally-driven story about vengeance and justice, all set within a unique and visually stunning world.
